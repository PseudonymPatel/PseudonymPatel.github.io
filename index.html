About Me

Making things is in my blood. I've been known to frequent the swift forums and other development forums. I'm interested in trying new technologies and pushing old ones to the breaking point. In my spare time, I've been developing exploring flight simulators and am currently building a working, reactive airplane throttle from an Lego Mindstorms NXT kit to use in a flight sim. I've also been working on Traincraft, a popular Minecraft mod.

If you ask others, they'll tell you I'm always busy learning something new. This summer, I've learned a ton of linear algebra and machine learning (not just Tensorflow, but the real math behind it all). I've taught some classes and taken some myself. A couple months ago I experimented with CTFs and expanded my low level computing knowledge.

Right now, I'm occupied with college applications (hi admissions officers!) and studying for standardized tests.

If you want to see some of my stuff, why don't you check out my GitHub or contact me on Discord (ComputerButter#8790).
