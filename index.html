About Me

Making things is in my blood. I've been known to frequent the Swift Forums and other development forums. I'm interested in trying new technologies and pushing old ones to the breaking point. In my spare time, I've been developing and exploring flight simulators and have recently built a throttle and joystick system for MSFS 2020 (check out the blog post here). I've also been working on Traincraft, a popular Minecraft mod. We are looking forwards to releasing the rewrite, which has been in the works for many years, soon.

If you ask others, they'll tell you I'm always busy learning something new. Last summer, I learned a ton of linear algebra and machine learning (not just Tensorflow, but the real math behind it all). I've taught some classes and taken some myself. A couple months ago I experimented with CTFs and expanded my low level computing knowledge.

At this point, I've finished my college applications! I'm working on marketing videos for Liftchart, a machine learning startup. School is going swimmingly, and I am enjoying a nice calm period in my life before I go off to university. Who knows, maybe I'll actually write a blog post. They are quite fun!

If you want to see some of my stuff, why don't you check out my GitHub or contact me on Discord (ComputerButter#8790).
